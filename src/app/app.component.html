HxSync

Connect your Standard Horizon HX890 portable VHF radio with a USB cable and click "Connect" to start. You'll be able to:
  • Read and modify waypoints
  • Download the GPS log in GPX format
Your device must be in "CP Mode". To enter this mode, first power the device off. Then, while holding the Menu key pressed, press the power button. Then, release both keys.
This experimental utility is not provided by Standard Horizon. Use at your own risk!
